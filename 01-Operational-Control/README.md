## Strategic Intent

This cluster focuses on capital protection, working capital discipline, and inventory governance.

Primary Objective:
Protect Return on Invested Capital (ROIC) through disciplined operational control.

---

## Core Risk Themes

- Dead stock accumulation
- Expiry loss leakage
- Working capital freeze
- Inventory vs cash conflict
- KPI manipulation (channel stuffing)
- Capital market exposure (SAIBOR shock)

---

## Decision Philosophy

Every operational decision must answer:

Did this increase capital efficiency without compromising supply continuity?

---

## Executive KPI Focus

- Inventory Turnover
- Dead Stock %
- Working Capital Delta
- Cash Conversion Cycle
- ROIC
- Financing Cost Exposure
