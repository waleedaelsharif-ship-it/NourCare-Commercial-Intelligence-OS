# Case 29 — Quarterly Capital Evolution Report

---

## Executive Summary

Following implementation of the Commercial Intelligence Operating System, NourCare achieved structural improvement in capital efficiency across four quarters. Working capital reduced by 46M SAR, ROIC improved by 3.8%, and cash conversion cycle shortened by 11 days.

---

## Context

Time Horizon: 4 Quarters  
Scope: National (48 branches)  
Objective: Measure systemic capital impact  

All prior clusters contributed to measurable capital evolution.

---

## Baseline vs Q4 Snapshot

| Metric | Q1 Baseline | Q4 Result | Delta |
|--------|------------|-----------|-------|
| Inventory Value | 184M SAR | 138M SAR | -46M SAR |
| Inventory Turnover | 5.8 | 8.2 | +2.4 |
| Cash Conversion Cycle | 74 days | 63 days | -11 days |
| Blended Gross Margin | 24.6% | 27.8% | +3.2% |
| ROIC | 16.3% | 20.1% | +3.8% |
